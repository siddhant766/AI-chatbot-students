📚 Academic Help & FAQs

Common questions about learning aids and chatbot usage.

How does the integrated chatbot assist me? ▼

The Noupe AI chatbot acts as your personal 24/7 tutor. It can analyze topics, explain scientific concepts, solve mathematics equations step-by-step, and assist you in study outlines.

How do I start a new session? ▼

Click on the + New Chat button at the top-left corner of the page. This instantly resets the chatbot iframe and opens a clean dialogue with the Noupe tutor.

Is my planner task list persistent? ▼

Yes, all study planner targets, Pomodoro settings, and notebooks notes are persistent. We store them locally inside your browser's localStorage, so they will be waiting for you when you return.
